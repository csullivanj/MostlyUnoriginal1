Why

  • I find almost all things interesting, and enjoy condensing varied sources of information about a topic into a structured, thorough format. My view is understanding anything is easier when you're aware of the landscape or map, i.e., what is the spectrum of opinions, research, and / or conclusions made on that topic? Apart from the essays, this is what this site aims to produce: the landscape.

    I usually share some findings once I think I've recognised I'm in the valley of despair trough on the competence scale. My work is mostly just borrowed ideas presented differently, hence the domain.

    In the words of Volatire, "originality is nothing but judicious imitation." I probably would've said "originality is mostly judicious imitation" but dealing in absolute terms is probably conducive to creating memorable quotes.
